Welcome, here is how to use this

This page has two tabs. The first tab saves a product, and the second tab shows the countdown and progress bar.

1
Go to Add product and enter the product name, purchase date, warranty years, and the seller rule if needed.
2
Click Save product. The item will be stored in your browser.
3
Open Countdown to see the progress bar, days left, and expiry date for every saved product.
