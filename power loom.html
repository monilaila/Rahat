About Our Village

Nestled in the picturesque countryside, our village has been a cradle of tradition and craftsmanship for over three centuries.

Founded in 1695, our community has preserved the unique cultural heritage passed down through generations.

Our craftspeople specialize in woodworking, pottery, textile weaving, and toy-making techniques that have remained unchanged for hundreds of years.
